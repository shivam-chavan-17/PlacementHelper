ABOUT US

PlacementStats was created by a person who care about the importance of placement for any student. This website is created in order to help the students who need the guidence for their interview preparation. This website connects the juniors with their seniors by providing their LinkedIn URL.

Our website is dedicated to providing you with accurate and up-to-date information on placement statistics for various industries and job sectors. We understand that finding the right job can be a daunting task, and our goal is to make the process easier for you by providing comprehensive placement data and selected student's LinkedIn URL that can help you make informed decisions.
